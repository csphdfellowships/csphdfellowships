CSPhdFellowships: Frequent-Asked Questions


Where is the data from? Is it accurate?

The list of fellowships is based on user-submitted or estimated information, which may be inaccurate, or out-of-date, despite the best efforts of its volunteers to ensure accuracy. Depending on your geographical region, the deadline may vary.

The deadline is wrong. What should I do?

Please open an issue and provide the updated data with the link to verify the same. We will update the data as soon as possible.

Can I contribute? How?

Everyone is welcomed to submit patches or report the fellowships information via pull requests. Another option to submit valuable datapoints is through this Google Form. Also, feel free to submit issues on GitHub.

What's your plan for the future? Will X, Y, and Z be added?

We have a list of items that we plan to prioritize in our efforts to improve this website. All contributions are welcomed. Please leave us a message if you want to implement one of these features.

Why don't the maintainers respond to my email?

We believe issues and comments should be discussed and resolved publicly on GitHub for transparency. If you believe any data is inaccurate or have additional comments, please open an issue or a pull request. The maintainers will not respond to private messages sent to their personal or university accounts regarding this website.

How can I make our organization's fellowship verified and obtain that coveted checkmark?

You may create an issue or fill this Google Form.

Why is the fellowship amount "NaN" or "varies"?

This is because we either don't know the exact amount of the fellowship or it varies from country to country. Please submit a pull request or open an issue if you have access to more accurate information.
